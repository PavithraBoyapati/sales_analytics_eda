
Sales Analytics Project | SQL • Excel • Power BI

Project Overview:
This project demonstrates an end to end Sales Analytics workflow using SQL Excel and Power BI.
The objective of this project is to analyze sales data to identify insights related to customers products orders employees and operations and present them through structured analysis and dashboards.

The project follows a real world analytics flow
-->Data collection
-->SQL based analysis
-->Excel based validation
-->Power BI visualization
-->Final documentation

Dataset Information:
Dataset type is a Northwind style sales dataset.

Tables included in the dataset are
Customers
Orders
Order Details
Products
Categories
Employees
Shippers

-->The dataset was first explored and validated using SQL before being used in Excel and Power BI.

SQL Analysis Summary:

SQL analysis was used to answer important business questions such as

1.Average number of orders per customer and repeat customer behavior
2.Customer segmentation based on order count and revenue
3.Revenue contribution by product and category
4.Geographic sales patterns
5.Seasonal demand trends
6.Product sales and revenue anomalies
7.Employee and operational performance

All SQL queries are written step by step and include conclusions for each analysis.

Power BI Dashboards Overview:

-->Dashboard one Customer Overview
Shows total customers total orders orders by country customer segmentation and geographic distribution

-->Dashboard two Orders and Time Analysis
Shows order trends over time monthly distribution average shipping duration and shipping company comparison

-->Dashboard three Product Sales and Performance
Shows total sales top products sales by category price distribution and shipping company sales

-->Dashboard four Operations and Employee Performance
Shows employee order handling performance trends employee distribution by title and operational insights

Key Business Insights:

-->A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue
-->Certain product categories consistently outperform others
-->Seasonal trends are clearly visible in product demand
-->Shipping companies differ significantly in order volumes
-->Employee performance varies by role and time period
